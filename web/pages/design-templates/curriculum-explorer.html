Assessment Framework
Skills Assessment
Can you do the work? Tool proficiency, technical execution, craft quality.
Knowledge Assessment
Do you understand why? Domain knowledge, institutional context, theory.
Performance Assessment
How well do you deliver? Quality, speed, reliability, consistency.
Product Contribution
What did you ship? Features built, bugs fixed, products improved.
Collaboration Assessment
How well do you work with others? Handoffs, communication, team impact.
Leadership Assessment
Can you guide others? Mentoring, decision-making, vision, influence.
Teaching Assessment
Can you teach what you know? Curriculum creation, tutoring, documentation.
Research Assessment
Can you discover? Original research, experimentation, evidence-based decisions.
Mastery Assessment
Have you earned it? Comprehensive evaluation across all dimensions.
