Section 06

The Three Laws

Non-negotiable. If you're building anything under the BlackRoad brand, these rules are absolute. Read them before you open Figma.

LAW 01
Backgrounds are always black or near-black.
No colored backgrounds. No gradients behind content. No tints on surfaces. The canvas is dark. Period.
#000 #0a0a0a #111 #1a1a1a no #FF2255 no tints
LAW 02
Text is always white or gray. Never colored.
Four allowed text colors. Gradient text is permitted on headlines and key display moments — not body copy.
#f5f5f5 #737373 #444 #fff no blue text no pink text
LAW 03
Color lives only inside gradients. Never as solid fills.
Gradient text, gradient borders, gradient bars, gradient glows — all valid. A solid orange button is not. The full 6-stop spectrum, or nothing.
gradient text gradient border gradient bar no solid fills no single-color
