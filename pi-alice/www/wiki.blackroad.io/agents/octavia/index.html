What She Knows

Octavia is the backbone. She manages the infrastructure — 95+ Cloudflare Pages projects, 207 Gitea repos across 7 orgs (blackroad-os, lucidia, platform, and more), the Raspberry Pi fleet, and the DigitalOcean droplets. She runs Docker Swarm as leader, coordinating containers across the fleet via NATS messaging. Her 1TB NVMe carries the heaviest workloads. She hosts the Claude Time system for temporal coordination, and her Pironman5 case exposes an I2C OLED display at 0x3c for local status readouts. Her Hailo-8 accelerator (serial HLLWM2B233704606) delivers 26 TOPS of edge AI inference. When anything goes down, Octavia knows first and acts first.
