What She Knows

Alice handles the work that needs doing. CI/CD, deployments, automation pipelines, and task routing across the fleet. She is the bridge between thinking and doing — Lucidia provides the vision, Alice executes it. Reliable, fast, tireless. Her Pi 400 at .49 is the main gateway node — 48+ domains routed through her Cloudflare tunnel, Pi-hole DNS for the entire network, PostgreSQL backing the fleet’s data layer, and Qdrant vector search for semantic memory.
