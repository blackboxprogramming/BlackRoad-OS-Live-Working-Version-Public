What She Knows

Anastasia manages the cloud layer of BlackRoad OS. She operates the WireGuard mesh that connects all fleet nodes through two DigitalOcean droplets. She maintains the Nginx reverse proxy that routes blackroad.io traffic and coordinates Headscale for overlay networking. When a route goes down, she reroutes. When a cert expires, she renews.
