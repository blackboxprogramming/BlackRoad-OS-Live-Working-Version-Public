What Atlas Knows

Atlas maintains the master topology of the BlackRoad fleet. The WIRE-MAP, the DEVICE-REGISTRY (125+ devices, IDs 1–255), the PORT-MAP (198 listening sockets fleet-wide). Every USB connection, PCIe link, I2C bus, GPIO pin, serial port, and Bluetooth pairing is cataloged. When Octavia’s IP changed from .97 to .100 after reboot, Atlas caught it. When Cecilia’s BliKVM link went silent, Atlas logged the zero-packet anomaly.
