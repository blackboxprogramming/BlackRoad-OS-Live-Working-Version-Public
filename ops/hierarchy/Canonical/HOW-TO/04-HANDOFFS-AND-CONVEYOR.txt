Handoffs and conveyor
=====================

Key concept
-----------
24-Conveyor-Belt is the queue before crossing layers.
25-Transfer is the actual crossing.

Interpretation
--------------
- 23-Ready-to-Move means the item is approved for handoff.
- 24-Conveyor-Belt means it is waiting in the handoff lane.
- 25-Transfer means it is actively entering the next layer.

Downstream rule
---------------
The receiving layer should usually begin at:
Workflow/01-Intake/

That creates a clean bridge:
23-Ready-to-Move -> 24-Conveyor-Belt -> 25-Transfer -> next layer 01-Intake
