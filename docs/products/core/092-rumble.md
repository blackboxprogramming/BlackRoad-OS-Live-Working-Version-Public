## Purpose
Restores presence and urgency without relying solely on visual noise.

## MVP Scope
Configurable rumble patterns tied to notifications, completions, or hazards via hardware vibration motors.

## Scale Mechanism
Embodied feedback scaling to any device with haptic capability.
