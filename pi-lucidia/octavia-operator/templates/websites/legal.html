1. What we collect

As little as possible. Here's the full list:

  • Account info — email address, name (Pro/Enterprise only)
  • Usage data — which features you use, crash reports (anonymized)
  • Payment info — processed by our payment provider, never stored by us

2. What we don't collect

  • Your AI conversations or agent outputs
  • Your source code or repository contents
  • Your API keys or provider credentials
  • Your agent memory journals
  • Tracking cookies or ad identifiers

3. Where your data lives

BlackRoad OS is designed to run on your hardware. Your agent data, memory journals, and configurations stay on your machines. We don't sync your local data to our servers.

4. Third parties

We don't sell data to anyone. We use minimal third-party services:

  • Payment processing (for paid plans)
  • Email delivery (for account communications)
  • Error tracking (anonymized crash reports)

You can request a copy of your data, request deletion, or opt out of anonymized analytics at any time. Email privacy@blackroad.io.
