About

We believe AI should
run on your terms.

BlackRoad OS is an AI-native developer platform built on one principle: you own your infrastructure, your agents, and your data. No exceptions.

The Story

Why we built this

BlackRoad started with a question: why does every AI platform want to own your data, your compute, and your wallet? We looked at the landscape and saw vendor lock-in everywhere. Per-token pricing. Per-seat licensing. Models you can't inspect. Infrastructure you don't control.

So we built the opposite. A platform where 30,000 AI agents run on hardware you own. Where no API key lives in agent code. Where memory is hash-chained and tamper-evident. Where you can deploy to Railway, Cloudflare, a Raspberry Pi, or all three with one command.

BlackRoad OS is not a product that happened to become a philosophy. It's a philosophy that demanded a product.

Principles

What we stand for

01
Your Hardware

Run on your Mac, your Pi cluster, your datacenter. We don't host your agents. You do.

02
No Token Tax

We don't meter your AI usage. Use local Ollama models or route through the gateway. You pay for the platform, not the tokens.

03
Tokenless Trust

Agents never see API keys. The gateway owns the secrets. Trust boundary enforced at the edge, always.

04
Portable Identity

CECE identity persists across sessions, models, and providers. Your agents are not processes. They are participants.

05
Tamper-Evident Memory

PS-SHA hash-chained journals. Every action logged, every chain verifiable. If someone changes history, you'll know.

06
No Lock-In

If you leave, you keep everything you built. The CLI, the tools, the agent configs. We earn your renewal, not trap it.
