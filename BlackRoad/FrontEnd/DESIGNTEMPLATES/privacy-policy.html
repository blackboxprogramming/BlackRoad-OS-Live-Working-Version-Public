Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours. We run on real hardware, not surveillance capitalism. This policy is short because our practices are simple.

Effective March 13, 2026

1. The core promise

BlackRoad OS runs on sovereign compute — Raspberry Pi nodes, Hailo-8 accelerators, NVMe storage, and WireGuard-encrypted mesh networking. Your data is processed on real hardware that we own and operate.

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train AI models unless you explicitly opt in. This is not a marketing position — it is a technical architecture decision.

2. What we collect

  • Account information. Email address, display name, and authentication credentials when you create an account.
  • Usage data. Page views, feature usage, and error logs. This is collected through our self-hosted analytics (no third-party trackers).
  • Content you create. Files, messages, code, and other content you produce using the Platform. This is yours.
  • Payment information. Processed through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

3. What we don't collect

  • We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.
  • We don't fingerprint your browser or device.
  • We don't build advertising profiles.
  • We don't sell or broker data to any third party.
  • We don't read your private messages, files, or code unless required for support you initiated.

4. How we use your data

  • To provide the service. Your content is stored so you can access it. Your preferences are saved so the product works the way you configured it.
  • To improve the platform. Aggregate, anonymized usage data helps us understand which features are used and where things break.
  • To communicate with you. Account notifications, security alerts, and product updates. We don't send marketing email unless you opt in.
  • To process payments. Stripe handles payment processing. Their privacy policy applies to payment data.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on our self-hosted infrastructure:

  • Primary storage: NVMe drives on Raspberry Pi nodes in our physical fleet.
  • Edge caching: Cloudflare Workers for performance. No persistent user data at the edge.
  • Encryption: WireGuard for node-to-node communication. TLS for all public traffic. Data at rest encrypted where supported.
  • Backups: Encrypted backups on separate hardware with limited access.

We follow security best practices: minimal access, audit logging, regular review. If we discover a breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

6. AI and model training

BlackRoad runs AI models locally on Hailo-8 accelerators and through Ollama on the Pi fleet. Your prompts and conversations:

  • Are not sent to third-party AI providers unless you explicitly choose a cloud model.
  • Are not used for training unless you opt in to a research program.
  • Are not logged permanently. Session data is ephemeral by default. Persistent memory is opt-in and user-controlled.

7. Data portability and deletion

Export: You can export all your data at any time through OneWay (our data portability product) or by contacting support@blackroad.io.

Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data. Deletion is real — we don't soft-delete and hold your data in a "grace period" indefinitely. When you say delete, we delete.

Portability: Your data is yours. We support standard export formats. No lock-in, no hostage data.

8. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session cookies. Required for authentication. Expire when you close your browser.
  • Preference cookies. Remember your settings (theme, language). No tracking.

We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

9. Children's privacy

BlackRoad OS includes educational products (Roadie, RoadSide) that may be used by minors. We comply with COPPA requirements:

  • Users under 13 require parental consent.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
  • Educational data is handled with additional care and minimal collection.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here and communicated through the Platform. We won't silently weaken your privacy protections.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@blackroad.io or open an issue on GitHub.
