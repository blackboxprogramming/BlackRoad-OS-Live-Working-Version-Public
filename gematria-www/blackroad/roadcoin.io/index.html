001 / Why Stablecoins Work
The Market Speaks Clearly

Stablecoins are the breakout use case of cryptocurrency. Not speculation. Not DeFi yield farming. Actual payments that actual people send to actual recipients. The numbers are staggering.

$6T+
Stablecoin Payments (2024)
More than Visa and Mastercard combined. Stablecoins processed over $6 trillion in transactions in 2024, primarily in cross-border remittances and merchant payments.
$195T
Global Cross-Border Volume
The total cross-border payment market is $195 trillion annually. Stablecoins currently capture 3%. Even modest growth to 10% represents a $19.5 trillion opportunity.
3%
Current Penetration
Stablecoins are only 3% of cross-border payments. This is pre-mainstream adoption. The infrastructure is being built now. RoadCoin is positioning for the 97% that has not moved yet.

Why Not Just Use USDC?

You can, and we interoperate with USDC. But USDC has a single point of failure: Circle. If Circle freezes your address (they have done this), your money is gone. RoadCoin is backed by a sovereign infrastructure that you can verify. The mesh network runs the settlement layer. No single entity can freeze your funds.

Why Not Just Use Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a store of value, not a payment rail. At $50+ per transaction and 10-minute confirmation times, it is impractical for micropayments. Lightning Network helps but adds complexity. RoadCoin settles in seconds, costs fractions of a cent, and is designed specifically for the payment use case.
