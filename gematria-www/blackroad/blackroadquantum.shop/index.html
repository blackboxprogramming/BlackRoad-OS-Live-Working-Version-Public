05 — Frequently Asked Questions

Questions and Answers

Do I need a Raspberry Pi to take the courses?+

Not for all of them. The Sovereign Computing and Blockchain Fundamentals courses work on any Linux machine, including a $5/month VPS. However, the Pi Fleet Setup and Mesh Networking courses are designed specifically for Raspberry Pi hardware. The Ollama course works on any machine with sufficient RAM (8GB minimum for 7B models).

Are these self-paced or scheduled?+

Completely self-paced. Each course is a collection of written modules with code examples, configuration files, and verification scripts. You work through them at your own speed. The monthly office hours (Builder and Enterprise bundles) provide live Q&A opportunities.

What format are the courses in?+

Written tutorials with embedded code blocks, configuration file examples, architecture diagrams, and verification scripts. No video content — we believe written documentation is more searchable, more maintainable, and more respectful of your time. Every module includes a "verify" script you can run to confirm your setup matches the expected state.

Can I get a refund?+

Yes. 30-day unconditional refund on all purchases. If the content does not meet your expectations, email us and we will process the refund. No questions asked. We would rather have satisfied customers than retained revenue from unsatisfied ones.

Is the certification recognized by employers?+

The BlackRoad Start certification is new and not yet widely recognized. However, it tests real skills — automated infrastructure checks against deployed systems. Any employer who understands DevOps will recognize the value of a candidate who can deploy and maintain a self-hosted computing fleet. The check scripts are open — you can show exactly what was tested.

Do the digital downloads get updated?+

Yes. Digital downloads are extracted from the live BlackRoad fleet and updated when our production configurations change. Builder and Enterprise bundle customers receive lifetime updates. Starter bundle customers receive 1 year of updates.

Can I use these configurations commercially?+

Yes, with one restriction: you may not resell the configurations themselves as a competing educational product. You are free to use all configurations, scripts, and templates in your own commercial infrastructure, products, and services without any royalty or attribution requirement.

How do I access the content after purchase?+

You receive a Gitea repository invitation with all course materials, configuration files, and verification scripts. Everything is in Git — you can clone, fork, and track your progress with commits. No proprietary learning platform. No DRM. Just a Git repo.
