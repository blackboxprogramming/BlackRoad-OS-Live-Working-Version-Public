Data Ownership Controls

Your data lives on your nodes. Period. No cloud storage you do not control. No analytics platforms skimming your behavior. Every bit of data has an owner, and that owner is you.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

All data at rest and in transit is encrypted with keys only you hold. AES-256-GCM for storage, WireGuard for transit. No backdoors, no key escrow, no exceptions.

👁

Data Visibility

See exactly what data exists about you across the mesh. Every agent interaction, every memory entry, every transaction. Full audit trail, full transparency.

🗑

Right to Delete

Not a request. Not a "within 30 days" promise. Instant, verifiable deletion across all nodes. Your data, your decision, executed immediately on infrastructure you control.

🛡

Network Privacy

Tor hidden services on 3 Pi nodes for onion-routed access. WireGuard mesh for inter-node communication. No IP addresses exposed. No ISP snooping on your fleet traffic.

Biometric Lock

Zero Analytics

No Google Analytics. No Mixpanel. No Amplitude. No tracking pixels. No fingerprinting. We do not collect behavioral data because we do not have a business model that requires selling you.
