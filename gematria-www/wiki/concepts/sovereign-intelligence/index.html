Concept · Local-First AI
Local Intelligence
AI that runs on your hardware, never phones home. Runs on your hardware, no API keys leaving your network. Tokenless gateway pattern. Your compute, your rules.
The Principle

Every inference runs on hardware you own. 16 Ollama models on Cecilia alone. 52 TOPS of Hailo-8 edge AI acceleration across the fleet. The tokenless gateway pattern means no API key ever leaves your network — agents call the local gateway, the gateway talks to providers. Ownership by architecture, not by policy.

tokenless://gateway:8787 → ollama://localhost:11434
agent → gateway → local model · no external API keys
How it works

Agents never embed API keys. All LLM provider communication flows through the tokenless gateway. The gateway holds the keys, the agents hold nothing. Provider switches are invisible to agents — swap from Ollama to Claude without changing a single line of agent code.

gateway.route — agent request routed to local provider
model.infer — inference executed on local hardware
token.zero — no API key transmitted externally
provider.swap — backend changed, agents unaware
Why it matters
Privacy
Your prompts never leave your network. No telemetry, no training on your data, no third-party access. What happens on your hardware stays on your hardware.
Resilience
Cloud goes down? You don't. Your AI keeps running. No rate limits, no outages, no deprecation notices. Infrastructure you own is always-on infrastructure.
Economics
52 TOPS of edge AI for the cost of hardware. No per-token billing, no monthly subscriptions. Pay once for silicon, run forever. Inference at the marginal cost of electricity.
The Philosophy

Your compute, your rules. Intelligence that depends on someone else's server is not yours — it's rented. BlackRoad OS runs on hardware you can touch, on a network you control, with models you can inspect.
