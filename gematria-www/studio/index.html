About

The creative wing

Lucidia Studio is where the BlackRoad fleet makes things you can see.

Lucidia Studio is the creative arm of the Lucidia AI system. It handles image generation, visual design, brand asset creation, and creative direction for the entire BlackRoad ecosystem.

Four generation engines compete on every prompt. Agents evaluate, rank, and select the best output. The result: production-quality visuals generated in seconds, stored on R2, indexed in D1, and served from the global edge.

Design Rules
01
Text is white or black only
White text (#f5f5f5) on dark backgrounds. Black text (#000) on light backgrounds. No colored text ever.
02
Containers are outlined, never filled
1px solid borders. No background-color fills on containers with text. Outlined or lined (top/bottom borders) only.
03
Gradient for decoration only
The 6-color gradient is used for shapes, animations, and decorative elements. Never for text, never for container fills.
04
Maximum border-radius: 4px
Sharp corners. No pill shapes. No rounded cards. 4px max on all elements.
05
Black backgrounds
Background: #000. Surface: #0a0a0a. Border: #1a1a1a. All backgrounds are black.
