Payment Types

Four Ways to Earn

Whether you create content, write code, or build communities -- RoadCoin has a payment model for you.

DP

Direct Creator Payments

One-time payments from supporters to creators. No minimum amount. No platform percentage. The full amount goes to the creator. Payment is recorded as a signed commit on RoadChain -- permanent, verifiable, and immutable proof that the payment occurred.

MT

Micro-Tipping

Small tips for content, comments, or contributions. Set your own tip amounts from $0.25 and up. Aggregate tips are batched for efficient processing. Supporters can tip anonymously or with their RoadChain identity. Leaderboards track top supporters per creator.

SB

Subscription Billing

Recurring payments powered by RoadPay. Monthly or annual billing. Tiered access levels. Subscribers get access to exclusive content, early releases, or premium support. Managed through the RoadPay dashboard -- no third-party subscription platforms needed.

BC

Code Bounties

Post bounties on GitHub issues or RoadCode tasks. Developers claim bounties by submitting pull requests. Payment releases automatically when the PR is merged. The bounty, the claim, and the payment are all recorded on RoadChain. Open source funding that actually works.
