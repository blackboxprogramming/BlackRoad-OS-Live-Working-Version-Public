# RoadCoin — "Your Cut"
*DI/Prose Interpretation style — Brand Ad*
*60–90 seconds VO | Emotional register: exhausted clarity — someone who finally sees the game*

---

## SCRIPT

---

The day they went public,

the CEO stood on a stage in New York

and thanked the community.

---

He said: *"None of this would have been possible without you."*

And then he rang the bell.

---

You watched it from your phone
in the parking lot of a job you still had to go to.

---

You had been on the platform for six years.

You posted three times a week.
You answered questions in the forums.
You made tutorials they featured on their homepage.
You brought your friends.
Your friends brought their friends.

Your content had been viewed —

you looked it up once —

forty-seven million times.

---

You made them.

Not alone. But *you* were part of the making.

---

Your cut?

Nothing.

---

That's not a conspiracy.
That's a business model.

*You were the product*,
so you weren't in the profit sharing.

---

There's a term for it.
They call it "user-generated content."

*User.*

Not creator.
Not contributor.
Not co-founder.

*User.*

---

Like a utility.
Like water through a pipe.

The pipe doesn't own the water.

---

RoadCoin is different.

Every post. Every build. Every lesson you teach,
every connection you make,
every problem you solve inside the BlackRoad ecosystem —

earns.

---

Not claps.
Not followers.
Not a vanity number that disappears when the algorithm shifts.

A currency.
Yours.
Transferable. Spendable. Real.

---

Denominated in your contribution.
Not their valuation.

---

The CEO rang a bell.

He thanked you.

---

Next time,

you ring it.

---

**RoadCoin.**
*The economy of the work you actually did.*
