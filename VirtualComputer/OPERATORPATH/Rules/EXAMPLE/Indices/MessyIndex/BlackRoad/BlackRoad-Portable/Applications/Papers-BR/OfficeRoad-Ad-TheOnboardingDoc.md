# OfficeRoad — "The Onboarding Doc"
*DI/Prose Interpretation style — Brand Ad*
*60–90 seconds VO | Emotional register: exhausted recognition → dark comedy → clarity*

---

## SCRIPT

---

On your first day,

someone sends you a document.

---

It is titled:
*"Tools We Use and What They're For."*

---

Slack is for quick questions.
Email is for external communication and anything that needs a paper trail.
Notion is for documentation.
Google Docs is also for documentation, but the *other* documentation.
Asana is for project management.
The spreadsheet on Google Drive is also for project management, but the *other* projects.

Loom is for async video.
Zoom is for meetings.
We also have Google Meet. Use that for the other meetings.

Linear is for engineering.
Jira is also for engineering, but only the old projects.
Don't touch Jira.

---

It is page four before you find out where to submit expenses.

---

There are fourteen tools on this list.

You have been at the company for forty minutes.

---

This is not chaos.

This is a business that grew.

Every tool made sense when someone bought it.
Every tool solved a real problem at the time.

The problem is that twelve tools,
each solving one problem,
creates eleven new ones.

---

The client email that needed a Notion page, a Slack thread, an Asana task, and a Google Doc
to fully exist.

The invoice that lived in QuickBooks
but the conversation about it lived in Slack
and the approval lived in email
and nobody is sure which version is current.

The decision that got made in a Zoom call
that wasn't recorded
that wasn't in Notion
that was mentioned in a Slack message
that is now six months back in a channel nobody checks.

---

*It didn't fall through the cracks.*

*The cracks were the system.*

---

OfficeRoad is one room.

Messaging. Docs. Tasks. Meetings. Files. Contracts. Invoices. 

Connected.
Searchable.
In one place.
Under one roof.
On your infrastructure.

---

New hire starts Monday.

You send them one link.

---

*That's the onboarding doc.*

---

**OfficeRoad.**
*Run your company from one place.*
