Working with layers
===================

Two questions define every item:

1. What kind of thing is it?
   That is the taxonomy layer.

2. What exact state is it in?
   That is the workflow step.

Examples
--------
A repository artifact under active work:
05-Repository/Workflow/16-Build/

A pull request artifact waiting for transfer:
13-Pull-Request/Workflow/24-Conveyor-Belt/

A comment artifact being preserved as durable knowledge:
15-Comment/Workflow/27-Archive-and-Reference/

Rule
----
Do not create new layers to represent state.
Use workflow folders for state and taxonomy folders for meaning.
