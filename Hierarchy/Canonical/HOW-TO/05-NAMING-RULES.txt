Naming rules
============

Directories
-----------
- Use numeric prefixes for order.
- Use hyphens, not spaces.
- Keep layer names stable.

Items
-----
Recommended pattern:
YYYYMMDD-short-name.item.txt

Examples
--------
20260415-auth-review.item.txt
20260415-worker-fix.item.txt
20260415-pr-handoff.item.txt

Why this pattern
----------------
- keeps chronological sort order
- stays shell-safe
- makes duplicate tracking easier
